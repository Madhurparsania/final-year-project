Science Gallery Bangalore

About the Museum

Science Gallery Bangalore is part of the global Science Gallery Network, bringing together science, art, and technology to create innovative exhibitions and public programs that engage diverse audiences.

  • Part of global Science Gallery Network
  • Located at Indian Institute of Science
  • Focus on science-art-technology intersection
  • Regular workshops and public events
  • Interactive and immersive exhibits

Location

Indian Institute of Science, CV Raman Road, Bangalore - 560012

Timings

Tuesday-Friday: 10 AM - 6 PM
Saturday-Sunday: 9 AM - 7 PM
Monday: Closed

HAL Aerospace Museum

About the Museum

Established in 2001 by Hindustan Aeronautics Limited, this is India's first aerospace museum. It showcases the history of Indian aviation and HAL's contribution to the nation's aerospace industry.

  • India's first aerospace museum
  • Established by Hindustan Aeronautics Limited
  • Over 20 aircraft on display
  • Flight simulators for visitors
  • Engine and component displays
  • Aviation history gallery

Location

HAL Airport Road, Near HAL Police Station, Bangalore - 560017

Timings

Daily: 9 AM - 5 PM (including weekends and holidays)

Ticket Information

Adults: ₹150 | Children: ₹75 | Students: ₹50 | Photography: ₹50 (Additional)

Featured Aircraft

MiG-21 Fighter Jet

Soviet-era supersonic jet fighter used extensively by Indian Air Force. Visitors can view cockpit and learn about its combat history.

Dhruv Advanced Light Helicopter

Indigenous multi-role helicopter developed by HAL. Features interactive cockpit access for visitors.

Aero Engine Collection

Display of historical aircraft engines including cutaway models showing internal working mechanisms.

Flight Simulator Experience

Professional-grade flight simulator offering realistic flying experience. Available for visitors with prior booking.

Museum History

Science Gallery History

Science Gallery Bangalore was established in 2018 as part of the global Science Gallery Network, which originated at Trinity College Dublin in 2008. The Bangalore chapter is hosted by the Indian Institute of Science.

Timeline

  • 2008: Science Gallery Network founded at Trinity College Dublin
  • 2015: Partnership established with Indian Institute of Science
  • 2018: Science Gallery Bangalore officially launched
  • 2019: First major exhibition "PHYTOPIA" opened
  • 2021: Virtual exhibitions launched during pandemic
  • 2023: Expanded to include AI and Quantum Computing labs

HAL Museum History

The HAL Aerospace Museum was inaugurated on August 30, 2001, to commemorate the 60th anniversary of Hindustan Aeronautics Limited. It was established to preserve and showcase India's aerospace heritage.

Timeline

  • 1940: Hindustan Aircraft Limited established
  • 1964: Renamed Hindustan Aeronautics Limited
  • 2001: HAL Aerospace Museum inaugurated
  • 2005: Flight simulator added for visitor experience
  • 2010: New gallery on Indian aviation history opened
  • 2020: Digital interactive displays added
